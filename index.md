# The Domino Effect

### A Mini-Course on Mathematical Induction

*Taught in the style of Richard Feynman — physicist, bongo drummer, safe-cracker, and the greatest explainer who ever lived.*

---

This is a gentle, thorough introduction to **mathematical induction** — one of the most powerful proof techniques in all of mathematics. No prior knowledge required beyond basic arithmetic and a willingness to think.

By the end, you'll understand how to prove that something is true for *every* number — all infinitely many of them — in just a few steps.
